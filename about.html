Callas - Most Reliable
And Trustworthy Company

CALLAS was started in 2013 and has quickly established itself as one of Hong Kong’s top suppliers of spare car and automobile parts. Our staff has grown to around 15 employees- amongst them are some of the most experienced Mechanical Engineers and Marketing Sales Experts in the country. Our reputation is strong; and we look to build upon it even further as we continue to grow.

Putting our customers first has enabled CALLAS to strengthen our reputation; and our innovative team of specialists ensures that all your present and future car spares needs are met to your satisfaction.

Although we are based in Hong Kong; we source second-hand spare car parts from all over South East Asia and beyond. Our eclectic selection of parts includes makes and models from some of the world’s most popular Japanese and European automobile manufacturers. We supply our parts to garages and aftermarket dealers from all over the globe at competitive prices- making us a strong competitor in both Hong Kong and the international stage.

Our customers are at the heart of everything we do here at CALLAS. Our expert team are continuously expanding our inventory and business- enabling us to keep up with demand from the ever-changing automobile aftermarket.
